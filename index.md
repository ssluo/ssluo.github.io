Hello! I'm Serena Luo, a recent graduate of Northeastern University who is passionate about data analysis and game theory.

Thanks to my work experiences across different industries, I have become at ease with all parts of the data analysis process--scoping projects, developing analysis methodology, coding scripts, writing documentation, and presenting results. At Wayfair, I dived into pricing databases and coordinated pricing projects with other internal teams. At Guy Carpenter, I gained experience with catastrophe modeling and improved the analyst workflow by automating data cleaning processes. At the Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority (MBTA), I conducted fare policy analysis and fare modeling related projects.  

![alt text](https://github.com/ssluo/ssluo.github.io/blob/master/assets/images/dance.jpg?raw=true)

In terms of personal hobbies, I do competitve ballroom dance and compete at the gold-novice level. I co-captained the Northeastern University Ballroom Dance Club during the 2016-2017 season and was invited back as a guest captain during Spring 2020. I organized travel to and from competitions, taught newcomer dance workshops, and ensured that all club dancers had the support they needed to compete and have fun while doing so.

Data analysis isn't just something I do at work - I love making projects out of regular things in my life. Check out [my portfolio](https://ssluo.github.io/portfolio/) for examples!
